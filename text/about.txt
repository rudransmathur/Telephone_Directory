Geo is a telecommunication service company which provides telecommunication services to its customers.
It uses a structured query language to store vast amount of records of its customers.
It uses GUI and visually rich application called Geo Enterprises which aids in accessing these tables.
Geo enterprises connects to 2 tables,
namely "Directories" and "Address" which store personal and postal address of the customers respectively.
Using this app, an employee can add, delete, update or search using constraints.
This app helps in easily manipulating and using data and is efficient in mangement of immense amount of data within seconds.
